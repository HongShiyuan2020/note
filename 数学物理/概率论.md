## 常见分布

### 离散分布
#### 0-1分布
$$
\begin{split}
定义: \\
&X \sim 
\begin{array}{c|cc}
  X & 0 & 1 \\ \hline
  P & 1-p & p \\
\end{array}
\\
&P(X=k) = p^k(1-p)^{(1-k)} \\
期望: \\
& E(X) = p\\
方差: \\
& D(X) = E((X-E(X))^2) = E(X^2) - E(X)^2 = p(1-p) \\
\end{split}
$$
#### 二项分布

$$
\begin{split}
定义: \\
& X \sim B(n, p) \\
& P(X=k) = C_{n}^{k} p^{k}(1-p)^{n-k} \\
期望: \\
& E(X) = E(\sum_{i=1}^{n} Y_i) = \sum_{i=1}^nE(Y_i)=\sum_{i=1}^n p = np \\
方差: \\
&D(X) = np(1-p)\\
\end{split}
$$
#### 几何分布

$$
\begin{split}
定义: \\
& X \sim G(p) \\
& P(X = k) = p(1-p)^{k-1} \\
期望: \\
& E(X) = \frac{1}{p} \\
方差: \\
& D(X) = \frac{1-p}{p^2} \\
\end{split}
$$

### 连续分布

#### 均匀分布

$$
\begin{split}
定义: \\
& p(x) = \begin{cases} 
\frac{1}{b-a}  &a\le x\le b \\
0 & other \\
\end{cases} \\
期望: \\
& E(X) = \int_a^b x\frac{1}{b-a} dx = \frac{x^2}{2(b-a)} \bigg|_a^b =  \frac{a+b}{2}\\
方差: \\
& D(X) = E(X^2) - E(X)^2 \\
& = \int_a^b x^2\frac{1}{b-a} dx - \frac{(a+b)^2}{4} \\
& = \frac{x^3}{3(b-a)} \bigg|_a^b - \frac{(a+b)^2}{4} \\
& = \frac{4(b^3-a^3)-3(b-a)(a+b)^2}{12(b-a)} \\
& = \frac{4(b^2+ab+a^2)-3(a^2+b^2+2ab)}{12} \\
& = \frac{a^2+b^2-2ab}{12} \\
& = \frac{(b-a)^2}{12}
\end{split}
$$
#### 指数分布

$$
\begin{split}
定义: \\
& X \sim e(\lambda) \\
& p(x) = \begin{cases}
\lambda e^{-\lambda x} & x>0 \\
0 & x \le 0
\end{cases} \\
期望: \\
& E(X) = \frac{1}{\lambda}\\
方差: \\
& D(X) = \frac{1}{\lambda^2}\\
无记忆性: \\
& P(X>s+t|X>s) = P(X>t)
\end{split}
$$

#### 正态分布

$$
\begin{split}
定义: \\
& X \sim N(\mu, \sigma^2) \\
& p(x) = \frac{1}{\sqrt{2\pi}\sigma} e^{-\frac{(x-\mu)^2}{2\sigma^2}} \\
期望: \\
& E(X) = \mu \\
方差: \\
& D(X) = \sigma^2 \\
\end{split}
$$

### 方差

#### 常见分布的方差

#### 方差的性质

$$
\begin{split}
&(1)\quad D(X) = E((X-EX)^2) = EX^2 - E^2X \\
&(2)\quad D(aX) = a^2DX \\
&(3)\quad D(X+a) = DX \\
&(4)\quad D(X+Y) = DX + DY + 2Cov(X, Y) \\

& \quad \quad \quad \quad \quad \quad \quad = E(XY)-EXEY
\end{split}
$$

$$
\begin{split}
L(\theta) &= \prod_{i=1}^n p(X_i;\theta) \\
ln(L(\theta)) &= \sum_{i=1}^n p(X_i;\theta) \\
\hat\theta &= \arg \max_{\theta} L \\
求法: \\
\frac{\partial L}{\partial \theta} &= 0 \iff \frac{\partial ln(L)}{\partial\theta} = 0
\end{split}
$$
